Australian TV Energy Consumption

This website explores the energy consumption of televisions currently available on the Australian market.

It is designed to support interactive data visualisations developed for COS30045 – Data Visualisation.

Television Market Overview

This section will display interactive visualisations showing screen technologies, energy usage, and efficiency ratings of televisions sold in Australia.

About This Project

This website was developed as part of the unit COS30045 – Data Visualisation at Swinburne University of Technology.

The aim of this project is to communicate energy efficiency information using effective visual design and interaction.
